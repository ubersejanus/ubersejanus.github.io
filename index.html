Hi, I'm Brad Peters. By day (and occasionally by night) I work as IT Technician, fighting the good fight to ensure that businesses across Victoria have the tools and knowledge they need to succeed and thrive in today's high-tech environment.

In my spare time, I am one of over 5,000 adult volunteers across the state who support the development of young people through Scouts Victoria. I'm an adventurous activities specialist, with a particular focus on abseiling; and also involved with delivering leader training to the Rover Scout section.

This website exists to corral social media accounts, show where I've been, what I've done, and also to host my blog. I've been blogging since 2006, but I'm not the most consistent. Mostly, I write about my travels and other things I've done or thought, stuff I've researched, and things I may want to refer back to in future. Hopefully it is of some use to others.

I live in Melbourne and spend time exploring, learning things, climbing, doing photography, and being on the internet too much.
